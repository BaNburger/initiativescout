```
Subject: Congrats on [Achievement] + Support Offer

Hi [Name],

Congratulations on [specific achievement]. I've been tracking Munich's student initiatives and [initiative name] stands out for [specific reason].

I'm building a support program for initiative spinouts, offering:
- Compute credits (GPU/cloud)
- Industry introductions
- Angel capital access

Would love to learn more about your plans post-[graduation/competition]. 15 min coffee?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Discovery Call Questions

For all calls, ask:

1. What are your plans after [graduation/current phase]?
2. Are you considering spinning out a company?
3. What support would be most valuable? (Rank: compute, intros, capital, space)
4. What's your timeline?
5. What's currently blocking you?
