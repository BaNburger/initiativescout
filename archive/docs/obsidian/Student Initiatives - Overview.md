# Student Initiatives - Deep Tech Startup Pipeline

## Executive Summary

Munich's student initiatives represent a unique opportunity for deep tech startup creation. These teams spend years developing cutting-edge technology while building cohesive teams - the exact ingredients for successful spinouts.

## The Thesis

**Student initiatives = Pre-built startup teams with proven technology**

Traditional startups face two major challenges:
1. Building teams from scratch
2. Developing technology while burning runway

Student initiatives solve both: they come with years of collaboration and working prototypes.
