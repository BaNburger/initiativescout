## Rating System

### Dimensions (1-5 Scale)

| Dimension | Description | Indicators |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| **Tech Depth** | Technical sophistication & innovation | Novel tech, patents, publications, complexity |
| **Team Talent** | Quality of team & leadership | Competition wins, alumni success, team size |
| **Market Applicability** | Commercial potential | Clear use cases, existing market, B2B/B2C fit |
| **Maturity** | Readiness for spinout | Years active, proven results, infrastructure |
| **Spinout Potential** | Overall startup potential | Combined score + qualitative assessment |

### Rating Key
- **5** = Exceptional / Ready for immediate support
- **4** = Strong / High potential with minor gaps
- **3** = Moderate / Promising but needs development
- **2** = Early stage / Significant gaps
- **1** = Nascent / Not yet ready
